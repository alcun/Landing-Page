About

  • Talkwell.world was founded in 2021 with the aims of helping students of English around the world reach their language-learning potential.
  • This is done by encouraging them to strive to improve in every lesson and seeing any mistakes as a chance to advance their knowledge.
  • We have found that students learn most effectively not from memorising textbooks and purely academic materials but rather from engaging with dynamic lesson content that is interesting and meaningful to them.
  • These resources can include:
    • Naturally Evolving Conversation,
    • Organic Resources e.g. Meeting Notes, Menus, Timetables,
    • Topic Related Videos and Podcasts,
    • Related Published Articles,
    • Art Media: Film, Music, Games,
    • Past Examination Papers and Marking Schemes.
